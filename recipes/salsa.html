Salsa

Description

This is a Texas recipe I've developed and refined over many years.

It's very fast, easy, and I usually have everything on hand. I often give it as gifts to my family back East.

Ingredients

  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
  • ½ onion, finely diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup canned sliced green chiles, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. 1.Place the tomatoes, onion, garlic, lime juice, salt, green chiles, and cilantro in a blender or food processor.
  2. 2.Blend on low to desired consistency.
